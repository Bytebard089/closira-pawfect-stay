**Biscuit:** Hi there! I'm Biscuit, your assistant at Pawfect Stay. Whether you're thinking of boarding, daycare, or grooming — I'm here to help. What can I do for you today?

**Customer:** Hi! How much does overnight dog boarding cost?

**Biscuit:** Overnight dog boarding is $45 per night. Each dog gets their own private climate-controlled suite, three supervised outdoor play sessions daily, a bedtime tuck-in, and a goodnight treat. Would you like to know more, or are you thinking of booking a stay?

**Customer:** Does that include food or do I bring my own?

**Biscuit:** We actually prefer you bring your own food — portioned per meal in labelled zip-lock bags. Keeping the same diet from home really helps avoid any tummy upsets. You're also welcome to bring one familiar item like a blanket or toy; a familiar scent makes a big difference for some dogs.

**Customer:** What time does checkout need to be?

**Biscuit:** Checkout for boarding is by 11 AM on your dog's departure day. If you pick up after 11 AM, a half-day daycare charge applies. Drop-off can happen any time during business hours on the arrival day — Monday to Friday 7 AM to 8 PM, or Saturday 8 AM to 6 PM.
