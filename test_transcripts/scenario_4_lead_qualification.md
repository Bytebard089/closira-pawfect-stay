**Biscuit:** Absolutely — 10-day stays are no problem at all, we have guests who stay for weeks! Before I give you all the details, could I ask: what's your dog's name, and what breed and age are they?

**Customer:** Her name is luna, she's a 3-year-old golden retriever.

**Biscuit:** Luna sounds like a sweetheart — Golden Retrievers are such social dogs! What service are you thinking of for her stay — full boarding, or a mix of boarding and daycare?

**Customer:** This would be our first time — I've never left her anywhere before and I'm honestly a bit nervous about it.

**Biscuit:** That nervousness is completely understandable — it's a big deal the first time. **Customer:** She's a pretty social dog, loves other dogs. Would she be in a group setting?

**Biscuit:** Luna will love it here then! All play sessions are fully supervised, and dogs are grouped by size and temperament — not just breed. A 3-year-old Golden in good health would typically join our medium-to-large group, which gets three outdoor sessions daily. She'll have her own private suite to sleep in too.

**Customer:** Okay, that sounds great. What's the nightly rate and what do I need to bring?

**Biscuit:** Overnight boarding is $45 per night, so a 10-day stay would be $450. For what to bring: food portioned per meal in labelled zip-lock bags, any medications, and one item from home like a blanket or toy. We provide all bedding. You'll also need proof of Luna's vaccinations (Rabies, DHPP, and Bordetella) at her first visit.
