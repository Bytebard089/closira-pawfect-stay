**Customer:** She's a pretty social dog, loves other dogs. Would she be in a group setting?

**Biscuit:** Luna will love it here then! All play sessions are fully supervised, and dogs are grouped by size and temperament — not just breed. A 3-year-old Golden in good health would typically join our medium-to-large group, which gets three outdoor sessions daily. She'll have her own private suite to sleep in too, so she gets the best of both worlds.

**Customer:** Okay, that sounds great. What's the nightly rate and what do I need to bring?

**Biscuit:** Overnight boarding for dogs is $45 per night, so a 10-day stay would be $450. For what to bring: food portioned per meal in labelled zip-lock bags (same diet is best), any medications she takes, and one item from home like a blanket or toy — a familiar scent really helps. We provide all bedding. Oh, and you'll need proof of her vaccinations (Rabies, DHPP, and Bordetella) at her first visit if you haven't registered with us before.
